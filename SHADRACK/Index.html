About Us

Many youth possess natural talent but lack certification and technical skills. We bridge this gap by providing hands-on vocational training.

Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality vocational training and mentorship that equips youth with practical skills, promotes self-reliance, and enhances community development.

Departments

Tailoring and Garment Cutting
Hair Dressing
Painting and Decorating
Computer Literacy

Curriculum

We follow the TVET curriculum approved by the Ministry of Education and Sports.

Our approach combines theory with strong practical training.

Target Group

  • Youth aged 18–30
  • Child mothers
  • Orphans
  • Other vulnerable groups

Advantages

We provide startup materials and equipment after completion, enabling graduates to start their own businesses immediately.

Achievements

Over 50 trainees have graduated and are now earning a living.

Future Goals (2026–2027)

  • Admit 100+ trainees
  • Expand training facilities
